<h1 className="text-2xl sm:text-3xl font-bold tracking-tight text-gray-900 dark:text-gray-100 text-center"> Taste of Research</h1>


<h2 className="text-2xl sm:text-3xl font-bold tracking-tight text-gray-900 dark:text-gray-100"> Call for Participation for Final-Year Undergrad and MTech Students </h2>  



Are you ready to contribute to building a safer digital world? "Taste of Research" invites final-year undergrad and MTech students to participate in BuildSEC'24, a premier conference focused on the future of cybersecurity and privacy.

This is your opportunity to be a part of groundbreaking discussions that transcend the technical realm, addressing the most pressing challenges in safeguarding our digital society. BuildSEC'24 brings together academics, industry leaders, and government officials to collaborate on innovative, real-world solutions.

The research tracks at BuildSEC'24 explore advanced security methods, privacy and trust, usability, measurement, risk analysis, and the broader implications of technology choices, including artificial intelligence. You can find detailed information <a href="https://buildsec.org" style={{ textDecoration: 'underline', color: 'blue' }}>here</a>.


<h2 className="text-2xl sm:text-3xl font-bold tracking-tight text-gray-900 dark:text-gray-100"> Why Participate? </h2>

- Engage with leading experts and thought leaders in the field.
- Attend insightful talks and workshops on the latest developments in cybersecurity and privacy.
- Participate in poster sessions where cutting-edge research is displayed.
- Network with professionals from academia, industry, and government.
- Gain exposure to a research environment, helping you explore future postgraduate or doctoral research paths.

While this opportunity does not include presenting papers, you’ll have the chance to immerse yourself in the research ecosystem through poster sessions, research paper presentations, and invited talks. This exposure will give you a taste of research, which is valuable if you are considering postgraduate or doctoral studies.

Join us at BuildSEC'24 and be part of the movement to build a more secure digital future for everyone!
